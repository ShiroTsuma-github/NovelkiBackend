The integration of diverse mythologies enriches the narrative, making it a delight for mythology enthusiasts.

The protagonist is exceptionally crafted, evolving from strong to stronger in a believable arc. Initial chapters have quite a few smuts, but don't let that fool you, this really offers a great plot. The story gradually shifts to an enthralling plot, a transition that enhances its appeal. The vivid action scenes are a standout, painting each battle with clarity and intensity.

Genji's inclusion of author notes and anime references like Naruto and Code Geass adds a personal and enjoyable touch. Although the pacing sometimes slows, it doesn't diminish the overall excitement. The pre-established relationships, initially confusing, are well-explained as the story unfolds.

In summary, this web novel is a compelling mix of genres, offering a rich narrative and well-developed characters. It's a unique, engaging read that goes beyond mere guilty pleasure. I'm invested in seeing how Sol's intriguing tale evolves.

Eagerly anticipating future chapters, and I highly recommend it. Thanks for writing, Hikaru!

An interesting read that will not disappoint you.

Reviewed at: CH 201: The hell?
by Wanderer_99 Friday, December 26, 2025 1:36:42 AM

The story starts of as straight smut(which becomes better and better) but the plot also progresses as a reasonable pace.

Some aspects are the same as usual fantasy isekai novels being that the main character is very 'blessed' and have some special powers with also being born as a prince. But the main points which differ it from other similar novels are the well thought out plot points, an interesting world that has many relations and references to various mythologies as well as very good r18 scenes...

The style of story is good too as many characters have a unique color scheme and it being a fantasy novel also has very diverse range of races and their distinct styles.

Story is great with a distinct plot which shows our mc struggling to live up to others expectations while also paving his own path. The whole story is well thought out and is well executed with clear progression.

Grammar can be a bit messy sometimes, but nothing serious. It does not have major mistakes and only minor ones here and there which will not be that big of a problem.

Characters of this story are very unique and have distinct personalities with good character development as well as great back stories too.

I do recommend this novel to someone who likes fantasy genre as this novel has a great story with very good r18 scenes as well. Don't let anything stop you from exploring this novel, the harem is good and feels real unlike other harem novels where they only have one character trait attached to them. It also has some great action scenes as well and one of the best things i like about this novel is the right usage of power by MC. He uses his OP powers the right way and author does not nerf him, which happens a lot in other novels.

P.S. i have caught up till the latest chapters and eagerly waiting for new chapters now...

 

Remarks : this review is about 2 years old now and at that time it was mentioned that story will end in about 6 months or so but author has started milking the story by making chapters shorter and slowing down the progress, that why it has degraded from it's original appeal. I may come back when it is finished but for now it will be in on hiatus for me.
